About WREN

Waste Recycling Environment Network, is a dynamic startup providing consulting services in the waste and recycling industry. From land-based waste to maritime sectors - our mission is to consult, educate and provide solutions to pave the way for a sustainable tomorrow. Proudly woman-led and embracing diversity, our team is spread across five countries and we communicate in ten languages. In the rapidly evolving landscape of growing economies, we confront the critical challenge of unmanaged waste head on and provide solutions deeply rooted in the realities of the communities we serve.
