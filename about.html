About Us

About Us

WREN - Waste Recycling Environment Network, is a dynamic startup providing consulting services in the waste and recycling industry. From land-based waste to maritime sectors, our mission is to consult, educate, and provide solutions to pave the way for a sustainable tomorrow. Proudly woman-led and embracing diversity, our team is spread across five countries, and we communicate in ten languages. In the rapidly evolving landscape of growing economies, we confront the critical challenge of unmanaged waste head-on and provide solutions deeply rooted in the realities of the communities we serve. We offer a scientific, practical, 'hands-on' approach to waste management and resource recovery technologies, training, community education, and awareness.

WREN’s Mission and Vision

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance the well-being of our planet. Every project we undertake works towards improving the planet and developing communities.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a B-corp, leveraging our profits for positive impact. We aspire to foster a zero-waste world through circular economy, resource recovery consulting and implementing smart waste management solutions.
