What We Offer

Education and Outreach

Empower and inspire your community through our education and outreach services. Our tailored education programs are designed to enlighten, engage and foster a deeper understanding of environmental issues and promote collective commitment for positive change.

Workshops and Training Programs

Our workshops and training programs are meticulously crafted by our seasoned specialists to equip your team with knowledge and skills required to navigate real world environmental complexities.

Needs Assessment

Unlock valuable insights with our comprehensive needs assessment, identifying knowledge gaps, challenges, and opportunities in waste management. Tailored to your target audience or organization, this assessment forms the foundation for effective strategies.

Training Program Development

Building on the needs assessment, we craft customized training programs covering waste reduction, recycling, composting, hazardous waste management, disaster waste, and proper disposal techniques. Our expertise extends to creating engaging educational materials, presentations, and interactive activities.

Outreach and Awareness Campaigns

Expand your impact with our adeptly designed outreach and awareness campaigns. From compelling marketing materials to organizing events and workshops, leveraging social media, we strive to raise awareness about waste management issues and drive positive behavioral change.

Stakeholder Engagement

In the realm of waste management, collaboration is key. Our team at WREN excels in fostering communication and collaboration among government agencies, businesses, community groups, and individuals. We skillfully nurture partnerships and encourage joint endeavors to promote sustainable waste management practices.

Are you seeking customized solutions to raise awareness, build knowledge, and foster sustainable practices?

Count on us to conduct insightful assessments, design training programs, and develop impactful outreach and awareness campaigns.
